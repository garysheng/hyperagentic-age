# winged-startup — generation prompts

Register: **plate** (from the `buildonanthropic-plates` story). Pass the plate
style anchor FIRST on every generation:
`universe/reference/register/plate-style-anchor.png`

Bake these plate rejectedPoles as hard negatives every time: neo-comic /
neon / 3D / CGI / Pixar / glossy plastic / HUD / arc-reactor vocabulary /
perspective / isometric / parallelograms / visible edge thickness or side faces /
shading / gradients / coloured or terracotta linework / ANY text or lettering /
storybook characters (Chief of Agents, sub-agent, Maya, Gary).

Invariant that must never drift: a hand-stacked tower of two or three flat,
slightly uneven cream blocks (the startup) with cream wings, reading as lift.

---

## hero  →  reference/winged-startup/hero.png   (the locked master)

Create a NEW illustration in EXACTLY the visual style of the reference image.
STRICT STYLE: single-weight wobbly confident BLACK ink brush line, hand-drawn and
imperfect; flat solid terracotta background; flat ivory-cream shapes as the only
fills; subtle paper grain. Face-on and flat, no perspective, no depth, no shading,
no gradients, no coloured linework.

Subject, the CANONICAL master, centered, calm, and upright: a small HAND-STACKED
tower of three flat cream blocks, slightly uneven and imperfect as if stacked by
hand, with TWO simple cream wings spread from its upper sides, lifting gently
straight up off the ground so it reads as airborne and about to climb. One clear
object, generous even terracotta margin all around, nothing else in frame.
ABSOLUTELY NO text, no letters, no numbers.

## detail  →  reference/winged-startup/detail.png   (load-bearing crop)

little off from the one below so the tower is charmingly imperfect, each with its

top corner. Fills the frame. ABSOLUTELY NO text, no letters, no numbers.
