## Premise

The story universe behind Gary Sheng's Anthropic wikis, explainers, and decks. It makes the
agentic-AI world **legible and warm**: it forks the SuperSuit *concepts* (Chief of Agents, the
agentverse, sub-agents, the hyperagent lever) and the *characters + paint register* of the
"get what you deserve" book, and renders them in a mature, humanistic editorial register.

## Characters

- **Chief of Agents** — the lead LLM-communication layer a person actually talks to. A small blocky
  clay-coral (Anthropic-orange) mascot, translucent and holographic, with a grand ornate **gold
  admiral hat** and gold epaulettes. Three states, only three: **listens** (one human input),
  **commands / oversees** (many sub-agents do the hands-on work), **delivers** (crosses the threshold
  once). Never does the task-work itself. Identity of record: `reference/chief-of-agents/master.png`.
- **Maya** — the founder at the human end of the crossing (forked from the book's Maya: rust blazer,
  lanyard, laptop under arm). A fictional composite.
- *(Forthcoming, not yet locked: the **sub-agent** — same mascot, plain white sailor cap, no gold;
  Gary himself, likely as a `realPerson` character when added.)*

## Doctrines (the craft-canon / visual laws)

1. **real-and-digital-law** — REAL = solid, warm gold-lit, casts a shadow. DIGITAL = translucent
   holographic Anthropic clay-coral orange (~#D97757), glowing, casts light not shadow. A hard,
   clear threshold separates them; they are never confusable.
2. **delivery-is-the-one-crossing** — delivery is the one moment a digital agent crosses into the
   real world, literalized as the Chief peeking out of a MacBook (mostly inside; only face + hands
   with the sealed work reach out). It happens exactly once per delivery.
3. **rank-by-naval-hats** — rank is carried by naval headgear only. Chief = grand gold admiral hat.
   Sub-agents = plain white sailor caps. The hat is the only rank signal, never size.
4. **warm-editorial-register** — warm, mature, humanistic editorial ink-and-wash (the book's
   register), forking SuperSuit's concepts. NOT neo-comic, neon, 3D, or glossy.
5. **master-first-workflow** — build every character master-first: lock one canonical master pose,
   derive every other pose from it (one per render), composite the sheet last. A pose that disagrees
   with the master is a defective render.

## Properties (stories over the canon)

- **anthropic-evangelism-wiki** *(stub, spine = thesis)* — the illustrated visual layer for
  anthropicevangelism.wiki. Render pipeline lives in that repo's `illustrations/SPEC.md`; this
  universe is the medium-neutral canon behind it (and behind future explainers and decks).

## Taste gates (where a human must say "that's it")

- **Words / concept before art** — lock what an image argues before rendering it.
- **Register lock** — the warm-editorial register is the point Gary signs off before mass production.
- **Master lock / face lock** — a character's master is Gary's call; derived poses must match it.
- **Subject approval** — any future `realPerson` property (e.g. Gary as a character) stays gated
  until the subject blesses it.

## Provenance note

Real people, if ever added as subjects, become `realPerson` characters with a photo stack, an
approval gate, and a sensitive list — and the property stays gated until blessed. No real person's
private detail ships in canon.
